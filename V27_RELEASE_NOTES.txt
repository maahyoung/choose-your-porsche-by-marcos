CHOOSE YOUR PORSCHE BY MARCOS — VERSION 27.0

Interaction refinement
- Doors and hood now respond on pointer release, not pointer down.
- Drag distance and gesture duration are evaluated before an interactive panel opens.
- Rotating the vehicle no longer triggers door or hood ghost touches.
- Exterior panel interactions are disabled while the cockpit camera is active.

Cockpit camera
- Added limited first-person look-around.
- Drag with mouse, touch, or trackpad to look left, right, up, and down.
- Movement is damped and constrained to keep the camera inside the cabin.
- Added an on-screen cockpit interaction hint.

Adaptive screen support
- Detects phone portrait, phone landscape, tablet portrait, compact landscape,
  desktop, and ultrawide layouts.
- Camera distance, target, field of view, scene offset, and platform scale adapt
  to the detected viewport.
- Added low-height and ultrawide UI refinements.
- Caps mobile pixel ratio and disables heavy shadows on phone-sized displays.

Lighting
- Reuses the GLB's dedicated LED/high-beam emitter materials.
- Increased headlight emissive output without recoloring unrelated materials.
- Added two real spotlights, local point lights, and layered flare sprites.
- The GT3 RS GLB does not expose separate mirror or rear-tip indicator meshes.
  V27 therefore adds independent amber overlay layers for both mirror repeaters
  and both rear indicator tips, avoiding whole-taillight flashing.

Scope
- GT3 RS remains the only vehicle in this release.
- Multi-model 911 support is planned after V27 interaction and lighting review.

Validation
- All TypeScript/TSX files transpiled with zero diagnostics.
- All local source imports resolved.
- GLB structure inspected for named light, mirror, and rear-lamp nodes.
- Full Next.js build could not be executed in the packaging environment because
  the npm registry was unavailable during dependency installation.
