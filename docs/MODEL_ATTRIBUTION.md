## Changes made

The downloaded GLB was prepared for browser delivery with meshopt geometry compression, WebP texture compression, deduplication, pruning, and vertex welding. Materials are adjusted at runtime for paint, wheels, brake calipers, headlights, taillights, and hazard lights.
