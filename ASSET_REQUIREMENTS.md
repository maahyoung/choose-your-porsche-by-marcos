# Final 992 GT3 RS model requirements

The final model is the most important external dependency in this project.

## Required separation

- body paint surfaces
- exposed carbon surfaces
- glass
- front and rear lights
- indicators
- wheels
- tires
- brake discs
- brake calipers
- rear wing fixed element
- rear wing active DRS element
- mirrors
- exterior graphics or decal surfaces
- license plate

## Technical targets

These are initial targets and may change after iPad testing:

- GLB/GLTF delivery
- sensible object and material names
- compressed textures
- limited material count
- no unnecessary hidden geometry
- Level of Detail strategy where useful
- geometry and texture sizes tested on an iPad before approval
- origin and scale normalized
- correct wheel pivots
- correct DRS pivot

## Licensing checklist

Before committing a model:

- confirm permission for public web display
- confirm permission to modify and optimize
- preserve required attribution
- store proof of license outside the public asset folder
- document the asset source and license in this repository
